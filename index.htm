Bio

HI! I am Siqiang Luo (骆思强 in Chinese). I am currently a 4th year Ph.D. student in the database group at University of Hong Kong, co-supervised by Prof. Ben Kao and Prof. Reynold Cheng. Before that, I got my bachelor's and master's degrees in computer science from Fudan University (with Prof. Shuigeng Zhou).

Research interest

I am broadly interested in data management systems, in the aspects of adaptive optimization techniques, distributed computation, and efficient graph indexes.
